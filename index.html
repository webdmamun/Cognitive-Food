About Us

Cognitive Food, in Bangladesh, has been trying to make their food more accessible to the customers by both creating value and opening new stores across geographical area. From the beginning of their journey in Bangladesh in 2021, Cognitive Food has opened 8 dine-in restaurants and 2 Cognitive Food outlets from where the valued customers can get their food delivered. Cognitive Food brand experience resonates generosity, friendships, naturalness and fun; making it stand for much more than the food.
